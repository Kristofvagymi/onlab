Visit Paris and France: excursions and activities for tourists

Paris City Vision is a sightseeing tour agency in Paris, France. Active since 1929, we offer a wide variety of guided tours in Paris and France's most attractive tourism regions. You can prepare your trip easily by booking the activities and excursions you're interested in online. With Paris City Vision, see Paris by day and by night and experience the unforgettable: Climb aboard a Seine River Cruise, see shows at the most renowned of Parisian cabarets, dine at the Eiffel Tower, buy tickets in advance for the Musée d'Orsay, benefit from a guided tour of the Louvre, and spend a day discovering the majestic Palace of Versailles. 
A number of Paris tours by bus and on foot allow you to explore the city from a unique angle. They let you visit Paris quickly, taking in its most breathtaking views, and seeing the city of light in a singular, new way. Do you want to visit Paris and the rest of France? We offer a selection of tourist circuits, activities, excursions and guided visits all over the country. Take advantage of several tours that depart from Paris to see the Chateaux de la Loire, the Mont Saint-Michel, Normandy beaches, and Provence.
Food-themed walking tours, guided visits, tourist activities, circuits, and all-inclusive visits let you see Paris and France in one or several days, offering you much more than an unforgettable vacation!
