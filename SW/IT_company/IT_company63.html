Leading ICT services companies in Mexico 2018

Published by Statista Research Department, Apr 30, 2020
The statistic shows a ranking of the leading information and communication technology services companies in Mexico in the third quarter of 2018, by revenue. The company with largest revenue in this sector in Mexico in the presented period was IBM. The revenues amounted to 7.65 billion Mexican pesos.
