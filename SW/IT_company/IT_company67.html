Mexico has great international relationships with major tech companies, soi much, that a lot of them wouldn’t survive without Mexico in my opinion.

Most Samsung smart TVs as well as LG and Sony TVs are made and assembled in Mexico.

IBM and Intel has invested a lot in Mexico this past decade.

In fact, a lot of the gaming industry is starting to invest in Mexico, so mucvh so that the average price of games went down from 1600 pesos to 900 to 1000 pesos and THAT’S WITH INFLATION!!!

Mexico has become very involved with the tech industry the past 2 decades.
