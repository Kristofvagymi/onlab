In addition to well-known international companies (Microsoft, IBM, HP, SAP, Oracle, Cisco, T-Systems and outsourcers like Accenture and KPMG), large national IT companies are Softtek, Qualitas-IT, Neoris, KIO Networks, Skanda Group, Ingram micro and others. The telcos Telmex, Avantel, Telefnica and Axtel also offer cloud services, software on demand and services for small and medium enterprises.
