If you live in California and you’re looking for a job, you have come to the right place.

What’s The Largest Company In California?

Concentrix is the largest company headquartered in California based on our database of 10 million resumes. It has 225,000 employees. See the full table or the methodology below.

Because, sure, you can google things like ‘biggest companies in California’ and get a fairly long list of companies with a lot of employees, or perhaps that have the biggest names — but here, we bring you the companies with the most current employees that are actually headquartered in California.

Below, we’ll give you a complete list of California’s 100 largest companies, but first, here’s a look at the top 10:

  1. Concentrix
  2. The Walt Disney Company
  3. Taco Bell
  4. Safeway
  5. Gap
  6. Apple
  7. Kaiser Permanente
  8. SYNNEX
  9. PORAC
  10. Intel

Concentrix comes in at the top, with more current employees than any other company in the state. We’ll get to specifics in a moment; first, here’s a look at our methodology.

How we identified the biggest companies in California

Here at Zippia we have developed a database of over 250,000 companies that spans the entire country.

For this report, we looked at the companies headquartered in California with at least 100 employees. That left us with 3,000 companies.

We then ranked each company from most to least current employees to determine the biggest companies in California.

As it turns out, Concentrix looks to the be the biggest company in California clocking in with 225,000 employees.

Now, check out how the 100 biggest companies in California ranked.
