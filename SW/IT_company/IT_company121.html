Corporate Connections

At HEC Paris, companies find what they are looking for: interns, young graduates, MBA graduates, executive education programs, professors to work with on research or teaching projects. Drawing from this positive experience, some of them decide to support HEC's development and become HEC corporate partners.

Join a productive ecosystem

Talented individuals from all over the globe come to HEC Paris to learn, be inspired, and find opportunities to make a more powerful impact in the world. Find new approaches to your business and recruitment challenges with us!

Experiment and co-create!

Build the world of tomorrow together in a unique partnership. Join one of our centers of expertise, create a corporate initiative, or a research chair around one a key topic for your business!
Find out more

Connect HEC Paris students with your brand

Strengthen your brand presence at HEC Paris and participate in campus activities to boost your recruiting efforts. Thanks to our privileged relationships with talent from around the world, we can connect your brand to future customers and recruits.
Find out more

Gain privileged access to talent at HEC Paris

Our team is here to support both students and companies. This dual role places us in a unique position to act as an intermediary between you and top talent from within France and around the world.
Find out more
