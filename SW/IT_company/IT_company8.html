Top 10 IT Companies from California, USA

0 Comments

You may have seen in science fiction films cars hovering in mid-air, the railway lines are laid at par with the high rises of a state or people are talking to robots, people are playing with them and other futuristic things. Some of these high tech things are happening in real life and some may happen in the distant future. We have robots today like Sophia who can talk like a human and even understand human emotions, the one thing which has kept humankind apart from machines. How was all this possible? The answer can be summarized in one word – Technology. Technology today has spread to such an extent that it has engulfed every single task that was earlier done by humans into its bosom. There are a number of IT-related corporations and enterprises housed in almost every country in the world, but very few of them have created an example which has astonished everyone.

In this scenario what’s a better example than California, the Golden State also called the home of Silicon Valley? Almost every mammoth company that we see today has its home in the state of California. It was California who gave birth to computers and the endless possibilities that came with it. The list of companies that were born in California is vast, but we are going to discuss just 10 of them today:

1. Apple Inc

there is no other option than to start the list with Apple Inc. The world’s most successful technology organization and the highest grossing organization is situated in Cupertino, California. This mammoth organization generated over $229 billion and employing 123000 employing. In the year 2018, it has become the 1st company in the USA to be valued at $1trillion. There are many worlds’ first and largest associated with this organization born in the year1976. The official address of Apple is located at 1 Infinite Loop, Cupertino, California 95014; the web address of apple is https://www.apple.com/, you can also call them at 1800 275 2273.

2. Alphabet Inc.

The introduction of this organization can be described as this “I had to search on Google to know about Google’s parent company”. Yes, Alphabet Inc. is the parent company of Google and it is also a major conglomerate that deals in technology, life sciences, R&D and many more. Although the Alphabet Inc. was created after a major restructuring of Google, the foundation of the organization was laid in the year 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin. With revenue that is more than the GDP of Morocco, Alphabet Inc. earns around $110 billion annually and it has the employee strength of 89058 in the year 2018. The official address of Alphabet Inc. is Google LLC 1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, California. You can also visit the official website www.abc.xyz to know more about them or just Google it.

3. Intel

You must have seen a small chip installed inside your computer, that chip is made by Intel. A world leader in chip manufacturing and processors Intel is only second to Samsung in microprocessor development. A 106 000 employees strong organization was established in the year 1968 in Silicon Valley, California. All the leading computer system developing organizations are Intel customers for microprocessor and chips. The 2017 revenue of Intel was more than $62 billion. The corporate head office of Intel is located at 2200 Mission College Blvd. Santa Clara, California 95054-1549. You can call them at (408) 765-8080 or visit the official website https://www.intel.in to know more about them.

4. Cisco Systems

this giant organization manufactures and develops equipment for telecommunications, for networking hardware and various other high technology services and products. It has also endeavored into the field of IoT and energy management and stepping into the science of AI recently. The 72000 employees have helped Cisco generate $48 billion in revenue in the year 2017. To go check out the Cisco Systems headquarters note this address: 170 West Tasman Dr. San Jose, California 95134, it is located in the heart of the Silicon Valley. For technical support, you can call 1800 553 2447 and for other queries, you can call at 1800 553 6387 or you can also check out their website https://www.cisco.com/ to know more about them.

5. Hewlett Packard Enterprise Company

the HPE is a part of HP Inc. and they separated their divisions in 2015. The HPE today manages the servers, storage and networking services where the HP Inc. is into personal computer and printers. Already a successful organization, after splitting apart HPE has brought further profit and generated more revenue than its parent company. It is also ranked above than HP Inc. in the fortune 500 lists of the largest organizations. The total revenue of the HPE in 2017 was $28.8 billion and the employee strength was 66000 in the same year. To know further about HPE, you can check out their website https://www.hpe.com. To go to the corporate headquarters follow this address: 3000 Hanover Street, Palo Alto, California 94304-1112 United States. You can also call them on 1650 687 5817 to talk to the customer representative.

6. HP Inc.

after separating its sister organization HPE, Hewlett Packard Inc is developing the latest printers and personal computers. They also major in producing the 3D printing solutions. During the period of 1998 till 2015 they HP Co. earned a combined revenue of $127 billion in the year 2011, the highest amount of revenue in the company history. After separating the HP Inc’s 2017 revenue is worth $52.5 billion. As of 2018 the employee headcount is 55000 and it was also ranked at number 61 on the Forbes 500 fortune list of 2017. The HP Inc. is headquartered at 1501 Page Mill Road, Palo Alto, CA 94304 P.C> 94304-1112 Palo Alto, United States. To contact them you can call on 1-650-857-1501 or visit their website https://www8.hp.com, to find out more about HP Inc.

7. Oracle Corporation

a major player in developing some of the best enterprise solutions, Oracle in 2015 was the second largest software maker in the world coming only after Microsoft. 41 years after its establishment Oracle Corporation has generated 39.8 billion dollars worth of revenue in the year 2018 and has a total of 138000 employees. Oracle has recently ventured into cloud computing and developing enterprise solutions for big corporate houses. The official mailing address of the organization is Oracle Corporation 500 Oracle Parkway Redwood Shores, California. For any other queries you can call on +1 650 506 7000 and +1 8000 392 2999 or you can also visit https://www.oracle.com.

8. Synnex Corporation

established in the year 1980, Synnex Corporation has its head office on Fremont, California. This mammoth organization has brought software solutions into logistics support and also supplies products to the IT industry related to peripherals and system components. The employee count as of 2017 was 107,400 and the organization collected $17.04 billion in revenue in the same year. The corporate headquarters of Synnex is located at 44201 Nobel Drive, Fremont, California 94538. To contact them you just dial 510-656-3333, you can also visit https://www.synnexcorp.com/, to know more about this organization which is ranked 198 in the Forbes 500 list.

9. Western Digital

California has a lot of the worlds largest and another one to the list is the Western Digital Corporation that was set up in 1970. This organization has expertise in developing data storage devices and technologies. The motto of WD is storing the world’s data, the solutions and products developed by WD for storing the data has no match in the world. With companies like SanDisk in its kitty now, the 2017 revenue of WD was around 19.3 billion dollars and the employee count reached 72000 in 2018. The headquarters of WD is at 5601 Great Oaks Parkway San Jose, California 95119. For technical support you can call at 1800 275 4932 and to contact with corporate offices call on 408 717 6000. The web address of the organization is https://www.wdc.com/en-um/.

10. Salesforce.com, Inc.

a major player in developing solutions from customer relationship management complemented by artificial technology for social networking sites and internal development. They are also the providers of various cloud storage solutions like sales cloud, service cloud, marketing cloud and community cloud. Setup in the year 1999, salesforce.com’s revenue for the year 2017 amounts to $8.39 billion and the employee strength is numbered 30145. The Corporate headquarters of this organization is located at The Landmark@ One Market Suite 300 San Francisco, California 94105. The contact information of Salesforce.com is: Tel- 415 901 7000, further the official website https://www.salesforce.com/ can tell you more about this cloud computing giant.

You can imagine the strength of this State by the size of its economy; the Californian economy has a value of over $2.8 trillion. If it were to separate from the United States then California would become the 5th largest country in the world in terms of economy. The World’s 10 largest companies are set up in California and moreover, 4 out of the 10 richest people on this Earth live there. What more can I say about California, the Golden State?
