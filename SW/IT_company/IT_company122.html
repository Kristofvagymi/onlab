Top 10 IT Companies from France

0 Comments

The age is digital and the time is the year 2018, what do you think are the possibilities of this modern age. The robustness with which technology has progressed has made the impossible possible. Everywhere you look there is at least one thing that is utilizing some aspect of technology that is developed by a team of inventors or organizations.
European countries are at the forefront of using technology for their gain and development with it. One such nation is France, which is the leading nation in the world for art and culture holding some of the most exquisite monuments and historical pieces in the world. France has 31 of the largest companies in the world and in various industries. The top 100 information technology industries in France generate a total of $7.4 billion in revenue for the nation. Here we have the list of top 10 IT companies from France:

1. Capgemini: established in 1967, Capgemini is one of the largest information technology consulting firms in the world. Spread in over 40 countries with a workforce of 1.90 lakh employees, it has generated revenue of 12.8 billion Euros in the previous year. They were awarded the world’s most ethical company in 2015 and also they co-wrote the 2013 world wealth report. The headquarters of the organization is in Paris, France; their address is Capgemini SE place de l’Etoile 11 rue de Tilsitt 75017 Paris. The contact information is as follows +33 14754 5000, for further information you can also visit their website https://www.capgemini.com/.

2. ATOS: another multi-national French organization headquartered in Bezons, ATOS specializes in serving almost every industry with their advanced services like cybersecurity, data management, artificial intelligence and many more. This 12.7 billion Euros firm is the official partner of Olympics Games since 2001 and has a workforce of 97 thousand employees. The address of the headquarter is River Ouest, 80 Quai Voltaire 95877 Bezons Cedex France, and the contact number is +33 1 73 26 0000. You can also visit their website to know more about the organization https://atos.net/fr/.

3. Sopra Steria Group SA: established in 1968 as a programming and analysis company, this software giant which has its head office in Annecy, France and having around 41 thousand employees today provides a range of IT consultancy services and ERP integration. Ranked 2nd in France among the top software developers in France, the 2017 revenue of Sopra Steria is 3.8 billion Euros. The official email id of the organization is contact-corp@soprasterai.com

4. GFI Informatique: is an organization which provides digital services to its clients. They operate in various areas like banks, infrastructure, and transportation etc. providing consultancy services along with cybersecurity, software solutions, and many more information technology related services. They are operating in 20 countries and have generated around 1.5 billion Euros in the previous year. With a workforce of 18000 employees, the organization has also obtained a gold medal for its CSR activities. The headquarter of the GFI Informatique is at 145 Boulevard Victor Hugo, 93400 Saint Ouen France. You can also contact them at +33 14404 5000 or visit their website https://www.gfi.world/en/.

5. Dassault Systemes: this is a European giant that develops 3D designs and has expertise in Product Lifecycle Management (PLM). With numerous acquisitions since 2011, the Dassault Systemes has become a multi-million Euro organization generating revenue of 3.2 billion Euros in 2017. The origin of this company was in 1981 by Avions Marcel Dassault who developed CATIA new generation CAD software. The Dassault Systemes which has a workforce of 16000 employees also has the French government as its shareholder. The contact information of the Dassault Systemes with its headquarters at 10 Rue Marcel Dassault 78140 Velizy-Villacoublay, France is: contact – +33 161 62 6162, for further information check the official website of Dassault Systemes https://www.3ds.com/.

6. Cegid Group: established in 1983 by Jean-Michael Aulas, the Cegid Group is involved in developing managerial software for entrepreneurs and companies. The organization is ranked at 20th position among the major software companies by the truffle 100. They have set their foot in providing software solutions to retail mega-chains and fashion brands. As of 2016, the cumulative of the organization is 476 million Euros. The head office of the company is in Lyon, France, and the postal address is 52 Quai Paul Sedallian 69279 Lyon Cedex 09 France. The official website of the company is https://www.cegid.com/en/, to get in touch you can reach them at +33 (0) 811 884 888.

7. Murex: the niche of Murex is in helping the financial market with technological solutions and advancements in big data handling and providing cloud management services to their clients. They have spread their wings into 17 major cities of the world, generating the overall revenue of around 460 million Euros making it the 5th largest software company in France in terms of revenue. The main office address is 8 Rue Bellini, Paris Cedex 16 France, and the contact information is +33 14 405 3200. You can know more about Murex by visiting their website https://www.murex.com/.

8. Cegedim: they operate in the field of healthcare and management, developing databases and software for this sector. Created in 1969, today the total workforce of Cegedim is around 4200 working to provide better and robust healthcare management tools and programs. The 2017 turnover of the organization is 457 million Euros and the headquarters are located in Boulounge-Billancourt, France. The official address and contact information of Cegedim is 137 rue d’Aguesseau 92100 Boulounge-Billancourt and the contact number is +33 (0) 149 09 2200. Their website https://www.cegedim.fr/Pages/default.aspx can help you know more about them.

9. ESI Group: the 1200 workforce organization established in 1973 develops tools and software for testing the product’s behavior and its real-life usage results prediction. The engineering industries use their services to detect any flaws in their designs. The 2017 revenue of the organization is 135 million Euros and they are currently operating in 40 countries around the world. You can learn about the services and software developed by them on https://www.esi-group.com/, and also contact them on +33 (0) 1 53 65 1414. The head office of the company is at 100-102 Avenue de Suffren 75015 Paris, France.

10. ISAGRI: the main focus of the company is in providing the farm with tools and software for administrative, technical, and economic issues management. Established in 1983 by Jean-Marie Savalle, today the organization has 1550 employees operating in 10 countries across Europe. They developed the first information portal Terre-net.fr for agricultural information dissemination in the year 1997. Their head office is in Tille, France and the official address is ISAGRI Avenue of Censives Tille France. For contacting ISAGRI call at 03 4406 4000 or visit the website https://www.isagri.fr/

France is going through a new wave of technological innovation and advancement, the organizations are demanding advanced methods of working and are ready to invest in the latest technological innovation with which they can achieve success. The Internet of things and cloud management systems provide some great potential to the organizations to drive them towards growth and expansion.
