These 7 companies are all hiring in Paris right now

by Eva Short

11 Feb 20196.34k Views

If you find yourself drawn to the ‘City of Lights’ like a moth to a flame, you should check out these companies hiring in Paris right now.

This month, we’re doing a site-wide focus on the thriving science and technology scene in Paris, France. If you have found yourself taken in by what the famous city has to offer, you may be considering taking the plunge and moving abroad to capitalise on the opportunities available.

Here, we’ve compiled a handful of some of the top employers in Paris currently recruiting across a broad swathe of industries.

Amgen

Life sciences titan Amgen has a Parisian hub that has been operational since 1990. It employs more than 350 people and operates in five different therapeutic areas: oncology, haematology, nephrology, osteoporosis and cardiology. It is seeking applicants for compliance, corporate affairs and regulatory roles.

Learn about Life at Amgen
Life at Amgen

Slack

Workplace messaging app Slack has been experiencing growth in the last while that is nothing short of exponential. It has recently confirmed plans to open an office in Paris, where it is hiring for a variety of sales positions.

Learn about Life at Slack
Life at Slack

Accenture

Professional services firm Accenture has offices dotted around the globe, including Paris. It is recruiting in the French capital for a variety of roles such as senior manager of digital projects, data architect, management consultant and more.

Learn about Life at Accenture
Life at Accenture

BMS

Bristol-Myers Squibb (BMS) has three French bases, one of which is in Rueil-Malmaison, a suburb of Paris located around 45 minutes outside of the city. The building was opened in 2002 and employs approximately 850 people. It is hiring in the areas of IT, management, quality control and more.

Learn about Life at Bristol Myers Squibb
Life at Bristol Myers Squibb

EY

EY is one of the ‘Big Four’ professional services consultancy firms. It has thousands of employees and a lengthy roster of clients, to which it provides a broad range of services.

At its office in Paris, it has a number of vacancies for different kinds of consultancy roles.

Learn about Life at EY
Life at EY

Dropbox

Dropbox has likely helped you out of more than one file-related jam since it was initially released in September 2008. It has locations dotted around the world including in Paris, where it is hiring account managers.

Learn about Life at Dropbox
Life at Dropbox

Mastercard

Payments company Mastercard has a variety of roles available at its Parisian branch. Not only is it seeking applicants for roles in consultancy, analytics and account management, but it is also offering one of its famed returnships, a 16-week programme that allows people who have taken a career break to return to work.
